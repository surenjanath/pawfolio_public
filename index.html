Privacy Policy

Last updated · July 2026

The short version

  • We do not collect, sell, or share your personal data.
  • Everything you add — pets, photos, notes, health records — is stored locally on your device.
  • No account system, no ads, and no analytics or tracking.

Pawfolio (“the app”) is a local-first pet organizer. Your privacy matters to us, and the app is designed so that your data stays on your device.

Information the app uses

Pet information & photos. Details you enter — names, breeds, dates of birth, photos, health records, journal entries — are stored only in the app’s private storage on your device. They are never uploaded to us.

Camera & photo library. With your permission, the app accesses your camera and photo library so you can add photos to your pets. Selected photos are copied into the app’s local storage and are not uploaded anywhere.

Notifications. If you enable reminder notifications, they are scheduled and shown locally on your device. No notification data is sent to any server.

Optional local AI (Ollama). AI features are off by default. If you turn them on and configure an Ollama server, the app sends the relevant text to that server, which you control — typically on your own computer or network. We do not operate this server and never receive your data.

Social waitlist (optional). If you enter your email on the “Social — Coming Soon” screen, it is stored locally on your device only. It is not transmitted to us or any third party in this version of the app.

Data sharing

We do not share your data with third parties. The app contains no advertising and no third-party analytics.

Data retention & deletion

All app data lives on your device. You can delete individual pets or all data at any time from within the app, or by uninstalling the app, which removes all locally stored data.

Children’s privacy

Pawfolio is a general-audience utility app and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Permissions we request

  • Camera & PhotosTo add pet photos — optional, prompted only when used.
  • NotificationsTo show local reminders for care dates and birthdays — optional.
  • InternetOnly used if you enable the optional local-AI feature to reach your own Ollama server.

Changes to this policy

If future versions add online features — for example, the upcoming Pawfolio Social feed — this policy will be updated to describe what data is collected and how it is used, before those features are enabled.
